About Our Church

City On The Hill Church International is a vibrant, multicultural community of believers committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ to all nations.

Our mission is to be a beacon of hope, shining the light of Christ in our community and beyond, making disciples of all nations through the power of God's Word.
