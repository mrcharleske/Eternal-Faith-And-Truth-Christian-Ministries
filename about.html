About Our Church

Discover our heart, history, and mission to shine Christ's light to all nations

Our Mission

To be an Impeccable Light in our community and beyond, faithfully proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ and making disciples of all nations. We are committed to Shining The Light Of Christ To The Nations through authentic worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate service.

As Jesus declared in Matthew 5:14, "You are the light of the world. A town built on a hill cannot be hidden." We embrace this calling to be a beacon of hope, love, and truth in our generation.

Our Vision

To see transformed lives, strengthened families, and thriving communities as we advance God's Kingdom locally and internationally through the power of the Gospel.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Biblical Authority

Authentic Love

We demonstrate Christ's love through genuine relationships and sacrificial service.

Community

Our Journey

God's faithfulness through the years

The Beginning

Eternal Faith And Truth Christian Ministries was founded with a vision to create a community where people from all nations could come together to worship, grow, and serve. Our founders felt called to establish a church that would truly embody the international heart of the Gospel.

Foundation Laid

Built on the Rock of Christ

Growing Community

Families and individuals joining

Community Growth

As our church family grew, we established various ministries to serve different age groups and needs. From children's ministry to senior fellowship, we created spaces where everyone could find their place and purpose in God's Kingdom.

International Outreach

True to our international calling, we began supporting missions and outreach programs across different nations. Our heart for global evangelism led us to partner with churches and organizations worldwide, truly becoming a light to the nations.

Global Impact

Reaching nations for Christ

Continuing Forward

Building for the future

Today & Beyond

Today, we continue to grow and impact lives both locally and internationally. With modern technology and traditional values, we're reaching more people than ever before while maintaining our commitment to biblical truth and authentic community.
